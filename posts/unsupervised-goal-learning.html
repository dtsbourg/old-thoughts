Open Review

What?

Instead of engineering a goal space, proposes to learn an unsupervised latent representation of the goal space using deep representation learning.

Why?

This work is an extension of the Intrinsically Motivated Exploration by Pierre-Yves Oudeyer and … Usually the goal space in engineered manually, which is not always possible, for example in high dimensional spaces where the achievable space is …

How?

Using deep representation learning to embed the environment in a latent space. KL-Coverage to evaluate the quality of the sample latent space.
